# Phase 10: Business Acceleration and Revenue Scaling

Status: Platform is secure and operationally stable. Focus now is revenue growth and market position.

## Primary Goal

Scale revenue in 90 days while keeping delivery quality and security posture intact.

## Priority Tracks

1. Enterprise Sales
2. Customer Expansion
3. Team Capacity
4. Compliance-led Positioning

## Recommended Execution Order

1. Enterprise Sales (immediate cash impact)
2. Customer Expansion (improves net revenue retention)
3. Compliance Positioning (improves close rate and pricing power)
4. Team Capacity (supports sustained growth)

## 90-Day Revenue Plan

## Weeks 1-2: Offer and Positioning

1. Finalize pricing tiers and enterprise package boundaries.
2. Define one clear sales narrative:
   - Problem
   - Why now
   - Why us
   - Proof
3. Prepare one-page security and compliance summary for buyers.
4. Set measurable targets in `BUSINESS_SCORECARD_TEMPLATE.md`.

## Weeks 3-4: Pipeline Build

1. Build target list of qualified accounts.
2. Run discovery calls and capture objections systematically.
3. Create proposal templates with security and SLA sections.
4. Track conversion rates from outreach to demo to proposal.

## Weeks 5-8: Deal Execution

1. Run weekly pipeline reviews.
2. Improve qualification to avoid low-probability opportunities.
3. Push for one lighthouse customer close.
4. Start QBR-style success checks with existing high-value customers.

## Weeks 9-12: Scale and Optimize

1. Document repeatable sales and onboarding playbook.
2. Launch referral or advocacy loop with satisfied customers.
3. Improve pricing and packaging based on win/loss analysis.
4. Set next-quarter hiring plan tied to pipeline quality.

## Metrics That Matter

1. MRR growth percentage
2. Pipeline coverage ratio (pipeline value vs quarterly target)
3. Win rate by segment
4. Average sales cycle days
5. Expansion revenue rate
6. Gross churn and net revenue retention

## Decision Framework For Weekly Reviews

1. Keep: activities directly improving win rate or expansion.
2. Fix: bottlenecks with highest revenue impact.
3. Stop: work that does not improve pipeline quality or retention.

## Risk Controls While Scaling

1. Do not weaken production security controls to move faster.
2. Keep incident response and security scans active.
3. Avoid over-customization for single customers.
4. Tie discounts to contract value and strategic relevance.

## Phase 10 Exit Criteria

1. Revenue growth trend is visible for at least 8 weeks.
2. Sales process and onboarding process are documented.
3. Expansion motion is active with existing customers.
4. Leadership can forecast next quarter with confidence.
