# Phase 9: Strategic Growth and Business Acceleration

Status: Security and operational baseline is in place. Focus now is measurable growth.

## Objectives

1. Convert security maturity into business trust and faster deals.
2. Increase delivery velocity without introducing avoidable risk.
3. Add a repeatable weekly and monthly business operating cadence.
4. Create clear scorecards to guide product, sales, and hiring decisions.

## 90-Day Strategic Sprint

## Days 1-30: Baseline and Messaging

1. Fill the baseline in `BUSINESS_IMPACT.md` and `REVENUE_MODEL.md`.
2. Set one primary growth target (for example, MRR growth or enterprise wins).
3. Align value proposition around "secure and reliable by default".
4. Start weekly business reviews using the provided template.

## Days 31-60: Pipeline and Product Focus

1. Build and track a qualified opportunity pipeline.
2. Prioritize features that improve retention or expansion.
3. Review scorecard metrics weekly and remove one major bottleneck.
4. Start monthly business metrics tracking in the CSV file.

## Days 61-90: Scale and Tighten

1. Standardize sales, onboarding, and handoff runbooks.
2. Raise deal quality by tightening qualification criteria.
3. Add a monthly leadership review tied to the scorecard.
4. Publish one customer proof point (case study or testimonial).

## KPIs To Track

1. MRR and ARR growth
2. Sales cycle days
3. Win rate
4. Churn and expansion rate
5. Gross margin
6. Team throughput (features shipped)
7. Customer satisfaction or NPS

## Operating Cadence

1. Weekly: run `scripts/business-ops-check.*` and hold WBR.
2. Monthly: update `MONTHLY_BUSINESS_METRICS.csv` and scorecard.
3. Quarterly: reset targets and re-prioritize roadmap.

## Exit Criteria For Phase 9

1. A working business scorecard exists and is reviewed weekly.
2. Leadership decisions are tied to metrics, not guesses.
3. Revenue and delivery metrics show positive trend for 8+ weeks.
4. Team has a clear transition plan into Phase 10 revenue scaling.
