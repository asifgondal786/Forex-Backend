# Thought Leadership Strategy

## Objective

Become a recognized authority in the product category and convert authority into pipeline and revenue.

## Positioning Statement

`Forex Companion` is the secure, operations-ready platform for teams that need reliability and compliance without slowing delivery.

## Channel Plan

## Content

1. Publish 4 core articles per month.
2. Maintain 1 pillar article per quarter.
3. Refresh top-performing content monthly.

## Speaking

1. Submit proposals to target events every quarter.
2. Track accepted talks and resulting qualified leads.
3. Reuse talks as blog/video assets.

## Community

1. Host a monthly practitioner session.
2. Capture recurring questions for roadmap and content.
3. Promote customer proof points and case studies.

## Media and Analyst Relations

1. Maintain a short list of relevant analysts and journalists.
2. Share quarterly updates: data, customer outcomes, releases.
3. Track mentions and backlinks.
