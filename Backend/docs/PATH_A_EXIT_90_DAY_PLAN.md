# Path A: Exit 90-Day Plan

Objective: Prepare company for a high-quality strategic process with clean diligence readiness.

## Days 1-30

1. Select M&A advisor(s) and legal lead.
2. Build diligence list and start data-room readiness.
3. Complete financial and legal cleanup priorities.
4. Define target buyer universe and rationale.
5. Align leadership communication plan.

## Days 31-60

1. Finalize information pack and management narrative.
2. Validate buyer list and outreach sequencing.
3. Dry-run management Q&A and diligence scenarios.
4. Close all high-risk compliance or contract issues.
5. Start controlled market signal testing through advisors.

## Days 61-90

1. Begin structured outreach to priority buyers.
2. Track inbound interest and qualification quality.
3. Prepare negotiation guardrails and red lines.
4. Confirm retention plans for critical team members.
5. Schedule board checkpoint and go/no-go call.

## Key Metrics

1. Diligence readiness score
2. Buyer interest quality
3. Time-to-answer diligence requests
4. Leadership retention risk
5. Revenue and margin stability during process
