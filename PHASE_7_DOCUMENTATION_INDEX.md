## Common Questions Answered

### Q: Is the system ready for production?
**A**: Yes. All 7 phases complete, 34/34 tests passing, security audit complete. [Verification](PHASE_7_VERIFICATION_COMPLETE.md)

### Q: What if something breaks during deployment?
**A**: Rollback plan available in [Deployment Guide](PRODUCTION_DEPLOYMENT_EXECUTION.md). Takes ~5 minutes to revert.

### Q: How long does deployment take?
**A**: 45 minutes total (Firebase 5 min, Backend 10 min, Frontend 5 min, Verification 15 min, Monitoring 10 min)

### Q: Do I need to be present during deployment?
**A**: Yes, for verification tests and to monitor first 2 hours. Deployment itself auto-runs on git push.

### Q: Is rate limiting already implemented?
**A**: Yes, in app/main.py lines 613-637. Phase 6 verified it's working. [Verification](PHASE_7_VERIFICATION_COMPLETE.md#rate-limited)

### Q: Are database rules already written?
**A**: Yes, firestore.rules is enhanced in Phase 7. Just needs firebase deploy to activate.

### Q: Can users lose data during deployment?
**A**: No. Frontend is stateless, data in Firestore. Deployment won't affect data. [Verification](PHASE_7_VERIFICATION_COMPLETE.md#frontend)

### Q: What if I find a bug after deployment?
**A**: Use rollback (5 min), fix bug, test again (30 min), redeploy (15 min).
